Why Sleep Matters

Sleep plays a crucial role in maintaining physical health, mental clarity, emotional stability, and overall well-being. Poor sleep habits can lead to decreased concentration, weakened immunity, and long-term health issues.

Adults typically require between 7 to 9 hours of sleep per night. Both insufficient and excessive sleep can negatively impact health. This tool helps you analyze your sleep duration and identify patterns over time.

This system stores your daily sleep data and compares it with previous records to determine whether your sleep quality is improving, declining, or remaining stable.

Healthy Sleep Tips

  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule every day
  • Avoid screens at least 1 hour before bedtime
  • Limit caffeine intake after evening hours
  • Create a dark, quiet, and comfortable sleeping environment
  • Engage in light physical activity during the day

These tips are general guidelines. Personalized suggestions will be generated automatically based on your stored sleep data.
