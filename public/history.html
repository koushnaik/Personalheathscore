Health History & Progress Tracking

Tracking health data over time is far more valuable than analyzing a single day. Trends help identify habits, improvements, and risks early before they become serious health problems.

This section is designed to display your historical data such as:

  • Sleep duration trends
  • Water intake consistency
  • Daily step count progress
  • BMI changes over time

In future versions, this page will visualize data using interactive graphs powered by Chart.js and data stored in history.csv.
