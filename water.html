Why Hydration Is Important

Water is essential for nearly every function in the human body. It helps regulate body temperature, transport nutrients, remove waste, lubricate joints, and maintain overall cellular health.

Even mild dehydration can negatively affect concentration, physical performance, digestion, and mood. Long-term inadequate water intake may contribute to fatigue, headaches, and kidney problems.

This water intake tracker helps you record your daily water consumption, compare it with recommended hydration levels, and observe trends over time using stored data.

General Hydration Guidelines

Body Weight Recommended Intake Notes
50 – 60 kg 2.0 – 2.5 liters Increase during physical activity
60 – 75 kg 2.5 – 3.0 liters Most adults fall in this range
75 – 90 kg 3.0 – 3.5 liters May require more in hot climates
90+ kg 3.5+ liters Monitor hydration closely

General Hydration Tips

  • Drink water regularly throughout the day
  • Do not wait until you feel thirsty
  • Increase intake during exercise or hot weather
  • Carry a reusable water bottle
  • Limit sugary and caffeinated beverages

Personalized hydration tips will be generated automatically based on your intake history and daily requirements.
